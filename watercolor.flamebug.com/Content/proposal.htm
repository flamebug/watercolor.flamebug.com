Domain & Hosting

Every website needs a domain name and a server to host it on, this section will go into the details on the requirements for hosting, and how the domain name will be pointed there.

Domain Details

Website Design & Content

This section goes into details on the goals of the website, the basic summary of content, the design, the navigation, and the details for each page on the website.

Goals

  • Allow potential clients that are looking for a body sculpting service to learn about Ultra Body's services, pricing, location, as well as provide a method of contact.
  • Maintain a modern website image that focuses on content with a well organized, easy to use interface

Content Summary

  • Copy text will be provided by Ultra Body. During site development, temporary content will be based on sonicthin.com until final copy is provided by Ultra Body.
  • Ultra Body's logo has already been designed, and will be used on the website as-is. Logo source files will be provided by Ultra Body as required to ensure a high quality logo is displayed on the website to all devices.
  • Any images/graphics (product, stock, before/after, etc) required will be provided by Ultra Body, and should be properly licensed for use on a commercial website.
  • Any required videos will be created and/or provided by Ultra Body, preferably via Youtube or Vimeo embedding.
  • Website code will be hosted on Github.com (a free service), and will be configured to automatically deploy updates to Windows Azure. An account will be created for Ultra Body on Github, and appropriate access will be delegated as needed.
  • Future website updates will be managed by Ultra Body, or may be re-estimated by Flamebug/Clint Kolodziej.

Design

  • Modern, clean, content focused, with a similar style language to existing Facebook marketing materials.
  • Neutral color scheme, black/grey/white, that allows content focus.
  • Fixed width, centered page layout, designed for modern desktop computer monitors
  • Optional responsive styles for the site template and each page's content to support small/medium screen sizes common on mobile phones an tablets (see responsive cost estimate).
  • Large company logo and header, with navigation, standard on each page for consistency
  • Content area below navigation/logo
  • Footer area below content, for common non-navigation details (email, phone, copyright, social media links, etc)

Navigation

  1. How It Works
  2. FAQ
  3. Pricing
  4. Contact Us

Pages

  1. Home
    • Hero section with large graphic imagery (similar to facebook marketing images), large text introducing Ultra Body's services, and a call-to-action button(s) linking to more information and/or the contact page to call or email for an appointment.
    • Informational section briefly explaining the Ultra Body services, and how they work. This would also have a link to learn more at the "How It Works" page.
    • Section to show a few of the most impressive before/after photos.
    • Section (if necessary) to show a few of the most important FAQ questions & answers, with a link to the FAQ page to list the complete FAQ.
  2. How It Works
    • Section detailing how the service works, who/what it is most beneficial for, and the benefits/results that can be expected. Additional information on how to prepare for the treatment, and how to care after the treatment would also fit here.
    • Video section to show relevant videos discussing the service.
    • Section to show additional before/after images (if applicable), or images showing details relevant to the service (similar to what may be in the videos).
  3. FAQ
    • Complete list of frequently asked questions and answers.
  4. Pricing
    • Table(s) of pricing information for various provided service packages.
    • Details on payment methods accepted.
    • Link to Contact page to allow the user to make an appointment via phone, or request an appointment via email.
    • Any other ancillary information regarding the services/pricing.
  5. Contact Us
    • Contact form that will send an email to sharon@ultrabodycontouring.com, with details on user's name, email address, and message details.
    • Address and phone number details for the Ultra Body location.
    • Google Map showing the location on a map.
    • Copy text indicating to call or email for an appointment, or for questions related to the services.
